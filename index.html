Image-to-Image Food Domain Transfer using Conditional CycleGAN


堀田 大地, 成冨 志優, 丹野 良介, 下田 和, 柳井 啓司


Daichi Horita, Shu Naritomi, Ryosuke Tanno, Wataru Shimoda, Keiji Yanai


The University of Electro-Communications, Tokyo, Japan.




Abstract:

This paper proposes “Food Image Transformation” based on the Conditional Cycle GAN (cCycle GAN)
with a large-scale food image data collected from the Twitter Stream for more than five years.
By the experiments, we showed that two hundred and thirty thousand food images
with cCycle GAN enabled very natural food photo transform among ten kinds of typical Japanese foods:
ramen noodle, curry rice, fried rice, gyodon, cold Chinese noodle,
spaghetti with meat source, white rice, eel bown, and yakisoba.
